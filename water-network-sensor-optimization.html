🧠 Problem Statement

In large drinking water networks, sensor placement is a critical decision. Poor placement can lead to delayed detection of faults or contamination. This project uses a combination of hydraulic simulation, probabilistic modeling, and Bayesian optimization to identify optimal sensor locations.

🔬 Methodology

  • Hydraulic simulation with EPANET
  • Monte Carlo contamination scenarios
  • Bayesian optimization for sensor selection
  • Multi-objective evaluation: coverage vs. cost
